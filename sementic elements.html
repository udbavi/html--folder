pollution

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances or contaminants into the environment, causing damage to air, water, and soil. It is primarily caused by human activities, such as industrial processes, vehicle emissions, and improper waste disposal. Pollution not only harms ecosystems and wildlife but also poses serious health risks to humans, contributing to respiratory problems, waterborne diseases, and climate change. Reducing pollution requires collective efforts to adopt sustainable practices, promote clean energy, and protect natural resources for future generations.
